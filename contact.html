Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about working with us

What happens after I submit the form?

+
Our team reviews your submission within 2 hours. You'll receive an initial confirmation email, followed by a calendar invite for a discovery call within 24 hours. We'll prepare a preliminary strategy based on your requirements.

What's your typical project timeline?

+
Most web projects take 4-8 weeks from kickoff to launch. Complex applications can take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines in our proposal and maintain transparent communication throughout the project.

Do you work with startups?

+
Absolutely! We specialize in helping startups build MVPs and scalable platforms. We offer flexible engagement models and have experience working with seed to series C companies across various industries.

What if I need changes during development?

+
We follow agile methodology, allowing for flexible scope adjustments. We prioritize changes based on impact and provide transparent quotes for any additional work. Regular check-ins ensure we stay aligned with your evolving needs.

What support do you provide after launch?

+
We offer comprehensive post-launch support packages including hosting, security updates, performance optimization, and feature enhancements. Our support plans are customizable based on your specific needs.
