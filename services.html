Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between a scan and a pentest?

A vulnerability scan relies on automated tools to find known flaws. A manual penetration test involves a human expert actively trying to bypass your security logic to find hidden vulnerabilities.

Will testing take our website down?

No. We perform safe, ethical hacking. We coordinate heavily with your team and usually perform intensive tests on staging environments or during low-traffic windows.

Do you help us fix the vulnerabilities?

Yes, our Detailed Mitigation Plans provide exact code fixes and configuration changes, and our Enterprise tier includes hands-on patching assistance.
